# Tata Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Effective Insights

## Project Overview
This project was completed as part of a virtual internship by Tata via Forage. The goal was to analyze business data and create meaningful visualizations to support decision-making.

## Problem Statement
Analyze business performance data and provide insights using visualization techniques for clients.

## Tools Used
- Power BI / Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization

## Key Tasks Performed
- Cleaned raw business data
- Created interactive dashboards
- Identified trends and patterns
- Suggested data-driven business improvements

## Business Questions & Analysis

### Set 1: Self-Framed Questions
1.Which countries and regions are generating the highest revenue, and where should we prioritize expansion?
2.What are the top-performing products contributing most to overall revenue, and can we scale these further?
3.What is the overall revenue trend over time, and are there any seasonal patterns impacting business performance?
4.What operational inefficiencies are affecting profitability, and how can we optimize them?
5.Who are our most valuable customers, and what characteristics define high-spending or repeat buyers?
6.How does customer purchasing behavior vary across different countries and demographics?
7.Which products are frequently purchased together, and how can we use this for cross-selling or bundling strategies?
8.What are the peak times (days, months, seasons) for customer purchases, and how can we align marketing campaigns accordingly?

###  Set 2:Client's Questions
1.What is the monthly revenue trend for the year 2011, and what seasonal patterns can be observed to support future forecasting?
2.Which are the top 10 countries (excluding the United Kingdom) generating the highest revenue, and how does quantity sold compare across these countries?
3.Who are the top 10 customers by revenue, ranked from highest to lowest, and how can they be targeted for retention?
4.Which countries (excluding the United Kingdom) show the highest product demand based on quantity sold, and what expansion opportunities do they present?

##  Visualizations and its insights
-Line Chart (Q1): Shows the monthly revenue trend in 2011 to identify seasonal patterns.
-Bar Chart (Q2): Compares the top 10 countries (excluding UK) by revenue and quantity sold.
-Bar Chart (Q3): Displays the top 10 customers ranked by revenue from highest to lowest.
-Map (Q4): Highlights countries with the highest product demand based on quantity sold.
